About

I am a Research Scientist at Singapore Management University, working on AI-driven wearable healthcare and health informatics. My research spans large-scale clinical data acquisition, biomedical signal analysis, interpretable and personalized AI/ML, edge deployment, and wearable device prototyping.

I received my Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering & Instrumentation Science from Zhejiang University in 2024, and was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (2024–2025), supervised by Prof. Yuanting Zhang (IEEE Life Fellow) and Prof. Ni Zhao. I have authored 17 peer-reviewed publications (7 as first / co-first author).

I am currently interested in foundation models for physiological signals, motion-robust wearable sensing, and translating research prototypes into deployable health systems.

Projects

Unleashing the Potential of PPG for Wearable Healthcare 2025 – Present

Singapore MOE AcRF Tier 2 (1M SGD) · Key Researcher. Building PulseLM, the first PPG-to-Text foundation model and benchmark for 12+ clinical tasks; CP-PPG, a GAN framework recovering distorted waveforms with +40% signal fidelity; and PPG-Sport, the first bilateral-wrist PPG dataset (48h) for high-intensity sports.

Cuffless BP Smartwatch with Multi-Wavelength PPG & ECG 2024 – 2025

Industry–Academic Collaboration (6.02M HKD) · Project Lead. Led a 10+ person cross-functional team and a large-scale clinical study collecting high-quality data from ~7,000 participants across 7 hospitals and community health centers. Delivered interpretable, lightweight AI methods for blood pressure monitoring and vascular condition identification. Contributed to 2 top-tier SCI papers.

Reliability Testing of Wearable Smart Products 2017 – 2021

National Key R&D Program (5M CNY) · Key Researcher. Built interpretable BP and core-temperature models from ECG/PPG/ABP correlations in human microcirculation. Adaptive calibration improved long-term BP tracking (SBP +36.6%, DBP +32.3%) and reached 92% CV-risk screening accuracy. Built ring-type wearable prototypes optimized on NVIDIA Jetson for real-time, low-power edge inference. Contributed to 5 SCI papers, 1 EI paper, and 3 patents.
